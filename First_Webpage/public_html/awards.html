Rotary Medalist

June 2013

About: These awards are presented to one male and one female student, nominated by faculty and voted for by fellow peers. The award criteria are as follows:

  1. Must be a graduating student
  2. Above average scholastic record
  3. Long-term involvement in school life (sports, clubs, activities, peer mentoring, leadership, etc.)
  4. Involvement in outside community activities that reflect the Rotary motto of "Service above Self"
  5. desirable qualities prescribed by the Rotary Club include cheerful cooperation with authority, desire for success in studies or play which is founded on the highest justice or morality, self respect, consideration for the rights of others, independence of character, readiness to forgive offense, evidence of practical belief in the Golden Rule, unflinching truthfulness and moral courage
